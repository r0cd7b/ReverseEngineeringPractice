LAG

Most common causes of lag:
  • Make sure that you have the most updated DirectX compatible video and sound drivers.
  • Close any fax, modem, or answering machine type programs.
  • Turn off Music and Sound on all systems.
  • With a cable over 10 feet long you can have signal loss.
  • Each adapter you use will increase the amount of signal loss.
  • Checking The COM port Settings
    • Click on Start, Settings, Control Panel
    • Double Click on "System"
    • Select the "Device Manager" tab
    • Click on the "+" sign next to "Ports (COM & LPT)"
        (if there is no "+" sign call your computer manufacturer to fix the problem)
    • Select the COM port that you are using for the Direct Link, and click on Properties
    • Select the "Port Settings" Tab
    • change the Flow control to "Hardware".
    • Adjusting the Bits Per Second: (Set and keep them equal on all systems)

      • If you experience lag (slow game with lots of waiting for other player messages), set the Bits Per Second as High as possible.
      • If you experience players being dropped during game play try lowering the Bits Per Second by one setting until you can play without players being dropped.
