MOUSE RESOURCES

To determine whether you have a serial or PS/2 mouse and the resources that it is using:
  • Click on "Start"
  • Click on "Settings"
  • Click on "Control Panel"
  • Click on "System"
  • Click on "Device Manager"
  • Double click on "Mouse"
    • This will tell you what type of mouse you have
  • Double click on the mouse type and then Resources tab
    • This will tell you the resources that the mouse is using
