UNABLE TO JOIN

  • This message will be shown when there are already eight players in the game. Or if the game creator has closed all remaining slots.
  • It is possible your Internet Service Provider (ISP) is having problems.
    • You will want to check with your ISP to make sure there are no conflicts on their end.
  • Try using a different Internet access number other than the one your friend is using (most ISPs have at least two access numbers).
